GOD of War

"I am Kratos, the Ghost of Sparta."

"I was once a Spartan warrior, feared on the battlefield, relentless in my pursuit of victory. In my thirst for power, I pledged my soul to Ares, the God of War, only to be betrayed—forced to slaughter my own wife and daughter. Their ashes cling to my skin, a curse that binds me to my past, a constant reminder of my sins."

"I sought vengeance, cutting down gods and titans alike, tearing Olympus apart until no god remained to challenge me. But rage is a beast that devours its master, and I was left with nothing but the echoes of my destruction."

"I abandoned Greece, seeking refuge in a distant land, the realm of Midgard. There, I found purpose once more—not in war, but in fatherhood. My son, Atreus, is my second chance. But the gods of this world do not suffer my kind to live in peace. They come, seeking battle, seeking blood."

"I will not seek war, but I will not run from it. For I am Kratos, and I have slain gods before."
